Kosgoda Sri Lanka is a small town in Sri Lanka's southern region, approximately 75 kilometers from Colombo. Kosgoda is gradually becoming a famous tourist destination in Sri Lanka due to its beautiful beaches, lagoons, climate, turtles, and turtle hatcheries. Prior to the invasions of the Portuguese, Dutch, and British, Kosgoda was linked with the production of cinnamon, farming, and fishing, which provided several trading possibilities to Sri Lanka.


Things To Do In Kosgoda

Kosgoda has a lot of intriguing things for us to do.

Kosgoda: Turtle Hatchery

Relax on a trip to Sri Lanka for individuals who love the beauty of landscapes and distinctive fauna. Visit the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, take a boat ride through the mangroves in Balapitiya, and take a Colombo city tour.

Galle To Kosgoda

This day tour will take you to the top sights on the southern coast of tropical Sri Lanka. Visit a tea plant, a turtle hatchery, a stilt fishermen hamlet, and many more attractions.

Udawalawe National Park
